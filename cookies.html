Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how CleaningRatio uses cookies and similar technologies for functionality, analytics, advertising, and affiliate tracking.

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They help websites function properly, remember preferences, analyze traffic, and support advertising and affiliate measurement.

1. Types of cookies we may use

  • Essential cookies: Help core site features work properly
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand traffic and improve pages
  • Advertising cookies: Used by ad partners to serve and measure ads
  • Affiliate tracking cookies: Help track referrals to partner sites
  • Preference cookies: May remember basic user choices if used on the site

2. Why we use cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep the site working reliably
  • Measure usage and improve content performance
  • Understand which calculators and pages users find most useful
  • Support advertising on the site
  • Measure affiliate link referrals and related performance

3. Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services used on CleaningRatio, such as analytics providers, advertising networks, and affiliate platforms.

These third parties may collect information according to their own privacy and cookie policies, which we do not control.

4. Your cookie choices

Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or limit cookies through browser settings. You can also clear existing cookies from your device.

Please note that disabling some cookies may affect website performance, analytics accuracy, ad personalization, or the way some features display.

5. Related privacy information

For more information on how data may be collected and used on the website, please review our Privacy Policy.

6. Updates to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect site changes, advertising setup changes, affiliate programs, or legal updates. The latest version will be posted here with a revised “Last updated” date.

7. Contact

For questions about cookies or site privacy practices, contact cleaningratio@outlook.com.

This policy is provided for transparency and website operation purposes. It is not legal advice.
