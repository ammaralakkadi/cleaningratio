About CleaningRatio

Six dilution calculators. Five topic guides. Dozens of practical articles. One goal: get the mix right without overthinking it.

Built for speed, clarity, and real cleaning situations.

What CleaningRatio does

Cleaning labels say "dilute 1:128" or "use 500 ppm" — and most people have no idea what that means in a real spray bottle or bucket. CleaningRatio turns those instructions into exact amounts you can actually measure.

Who uses this

Anyone mixing a cleaning solution — homeowners, renters, parents, cleaners, janitors, restaurant staff, daycare workers, and small business owners. If you've ever stared at a label and guessed, this site exists so you don't have to.

How the site is built

Calculators first, guides second, articles third. Every page exists to answer a specific cleaning question as fast as possible. No accounts, no popups, no paywalls. The site loads fast because it's built to be lightweight by design — not as an afterthought.

What we cover

Bleach dilution, vinegar cleaning ratios, essential oil mixing, hydrogen peroxide dilution, hypochlorous acid, ppm calculations, surface-specific guides, and the dilution math that connects them all. Each topic has a hub page that ties the calculator, reference tables, and articles together.

Important disclaimer

Calculator results are based on standard dilution formulas. They do not replace product labels, manufacturer instructions, or professional guidance — especially for strong chemicals, sensitive surfaces, or regulated environments. Always read the label first.
