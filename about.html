About CleaningRatio

Practical cleaning dilution calculators and mixing guides designed to be clear, fast, and reliable.

Built for real use — not clutter, not guesswork.

What we do

CleaningRatio helps people mix cleaning products correctly using simple calculators and easy-to-follow guidance. The goal is to reduce confusion, save time, and support safer day-to-day cleaning decisions.

Who this is for

Home users, cleaning staff, small businesses, and anyone who needs quick dilution ratios without complicated charts.

Why this site exists

Many cleaning labels and ratio instructions are written in a way that feels harder than it should be. This site focuses on clean tools and practical examples so users can get the answer quickly and move on with confidence.

What makes CleaningRatio different

We focus on clarity, readability, and a calm user experience. No unnecessary complexity. No distracting design. Just useful calculators and guidance that respect your time.

Calculators and guides

Alongside calculators, CleaningRatio also publishes blog articles and mixing guides covering common cleaning situations, practical tips, and safer usage reminders for home and commercial environments.

Important note

Calculator results are general guidance. Always check the product label, manufacturer instructions, and surface compatibility before use, especially for strong chemicals or sensitive materials.

Partnerships and feedback

We welcome affiliate partnerships, product-related inquiries, and user feedback that helps improve the site.
