Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information CleaningRatio may collect, how it is used, and what users can expect when using the website.

Last updated: February 22, 2026

CleaningRatio provides cleaning dilution calculators, mixing guides, and related content for general informational use. We aim to collect as little personal information as reasonably possible while keeping the site useful, secure, and easy to use.

1. Information we may collect

Depending on how you use the site, we may collect:

  • Basic usage data such as pages viewed and time on page
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address (typically through hosting, analytics, or ad partners)
  • Information you send directly if you contact us by email or contact form
  • Cookie and similar technology data used for analytics, advertising, and site functionality

2. How we use information

We may use collected information to:

  • Operate and improve the website and calculators
  • Understand how visitors use the site
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse
  • Respond to messages, support, or partnership inquiries
  • Serve advertisements and measure ad performance (including through third-party ad providers)
  • Track affiliate link performance and related referral activity

3. Advertising and affiliate links

CleaningRatio displays advertisements and includes affiliate links. This means we may earn commissions or advertising revenue when users click certain links or make purchases through partner websites.

Clicking an affiliate link may allow a third party to recognize that traffic came from CleaningRatio. Those third-party sites and services may use their own cookies, tracking technologies, and privacy policies.

We do not control third-party websites, merchants, ad networks, or their privacy practices. Please review their policies before sharing information or completing purchases.

4. Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for site performance, analytics, advertising, and affiliate tracking. Some cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly, while others help us understand traffic and improve content.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect parts of the site experience.

For more detail, please review our Cookie Policy.

5. Sharing of information

We may share information with service providers or partners that help operate the site, such as:

  • Website hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Analytics providers
  • Advertising partners and networks
  • Affiliate platforms and merchant programs
  • Security and anti-abuse tools

We may also disclose information when required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of the site, users, or others.

6. Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including operating the site, maintaining records, complying with legal obligations, and resolving disputes.

7. International users

CleaningRatio is available to users worldwide. By using the site, you understand that information may be processed in countries other than your own, where data protection laws may differ.

We aim to handle information responsibly and in a practical, security-conscious manner appropriate for a small informational website.

8. Your choices

You may:

  • Manage cookies in your browser settings
  • Avoid submitting personal information through forms or email
  • Contact us to request privacy-related support or questions

9. Children’s privacy

CleaningRatio is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect site changes, legal updates, or advertising and affiliate program requirements. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, partnership inquiries, or general support, contact us at cleaningratio@outlook.com.

This policy is provided for general transparency and website operation purposes. It is not legal advice.
