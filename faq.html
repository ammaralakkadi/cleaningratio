FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about calculator use, dilution guidance, blog content, and general site information.

What does CleaningRatio help with?

CleaningRatio provides practical dilution calculators and cleaning ratio guidance to help users mix products more clearly and quickly.

Are the calculator results exact for every product?

Calculator results are general guidance based on ratio inputs. Always check the product label and manufacturer instructions first, because concentration and use cases can vary.

Can I use the calculators for household and commercial cleaning?

Yes, the calculators are designed to be useful for both home and professional cleaning scenarios. The important step is matching the ratio to the product instructions you are using.

Do you publish blog articles or guides?

Yes. CleaningRatio includes calculators and also publishes blog articles and mixing guides focused on practical cleaning situations, usage reminders, and clearer instructions.

Can I contact you for partnerships or affiliate opportunities?

Yes. Partnership and affiliate inquiries are welcome. Please use the Contact page or email cleaningratio@outlook.com.

Why is the website designed to be simple?

The site is intentionally built for clarity and speed. The goal is to help users get accurate ratio help fast, without distracting layouts or unnecessary steps.

What should I do before using any cleaning solution?

Read the product label, confirm the intended surface or material, and follow safety instructions from the manufacturer. This is especially important for stronger chemicals and sensitive surfaces.
